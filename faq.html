Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How does the app work?

The app connects restaurants with NGOs to redistribute surplus food to communities in need.

What types of food can be donated?

Restaurants can donate any unsold, safe, and non-perishable food items.

How do I request a pickup?

NGOs can view available food donations and request pickups through the app.
